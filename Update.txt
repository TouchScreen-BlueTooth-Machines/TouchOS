      ===| New Update! |===

TouchOS 6.8 has just been released!
This new version of TouchOS is set
to be supported until around 2026.
This means that TouchOS 6.8x will be
recieving updates from the company
until Jan 1st 2026. This is the same
day that TouchOS Server 21 R2 will
also be losing support. TouchOS 6.8
is probably going to be the last
  release of TouchOS ever.
